Thursday 3rd August

LE PARADIS↪


OPENING FILM

Thursday 3rd August
20:00
Light House

 Dir. Zeno Graton
Belgium/France, 2023
1h 23m

We have a real dynamite opener to make a start to GAZE 2023. Young love, lust, and law-breaking rear their heads in Zeno Graton's incredible taut character-driven French/Belgian gay drama, Le Paradis, aka The Lost Boys. The film contrasts the restless claustrophobia of a young men's detention centre with the boundless thrills of first love, and young fantasies of the future, producing a story that is filled with yearning, complexity and humour. The two central performances, wayward lads Joe (Khalil Gharbia) and William (Julian de Saint Jean), underpin what is truly a sensational ensemble cast effort. It's hard to believe this is Graton's feature film debut, so accomplished is the world contained in his film. Will love save the day in this explosive world of friction, fighting and fragile masculinity? Le Paradis brought the house down at the Berlinale and we hope it will do the same here in Dublin for our Opening Gala event of 2023. Welcome back to GAZE in August!
